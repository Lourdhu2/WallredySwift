# Privacy Policy for Swyftwall

**Last updated: April 3, 2026**

## Overview

Swyftwall ("the App") is a wallpaper browsing application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data Collection

Swyftwall does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- We do not require account creation or login
- We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
- We do not track your location
- We do not use analytics or tracking tools
- We do not use cookies
- We do not collect device identifiers

## Photos Library Access

The App requests access to your photo library solely to save wallpapers that you choose to download. This access is only used when you tap the save button. No photos are uploaded or shared from your device.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the **Pexels API** (https://www.pexels.com) to display wallpaper images. When you browse wallpapers, your device connects to Pexels servers to load images. Pexels has its own privacy policy available at https://www.pexels.com/privacy-policy/.

## Data Storage

All favorites and preferences are stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers because we do not operate any servers.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
